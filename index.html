Research University

With 48 research units and a highly qualified scientific
community, the University of Porto is one of the largest
producers of science in Portugal, contributing to almost 25% of
all national scientific output.

The excellence of our scientific ecosystem is particularly evident
in the leading position the University occupies in the main
international rankings of Higher Education.
